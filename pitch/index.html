Healthee Value Proposition

The Problem

The Health Tech Landscape is Broken

Current solutions focus on raw metrics, rigid shaming patterns, and complex diagnostics without clear guidance.

Rigid Streak Guilt

Static calendars declare you a "failure" if you miss a single day, ignoring life's irregular schedules and sicknesses.

Metric Toxicity

Obsession with scale weight, calorie counting, and intense restrictive diets leads to burnout and stress.

The Innovation

Dynamic Phase-Based Rhythms

Healthee replaces weekly checklists with a flexible structure of 1 to 6 phases. It adapts directly to the user's current baseline.

Observation First

If a user lacks clarity about their baseline, the AI schedules a low-friction phase to simply observe patterns before taking action.

Autopilot Adaptation

Custom-built mathematical signals and transition rules let the app simplify, repeat, or progress phases automatically.

Technical Architecture

The AI Adaptive State Machine

A secure LLM engine maps onboarding, diagnostics, and daily check-ins into structured JSON plan models.

1. Onboarding Vitals
→
2. LLM Engine (JSON Schema)
→
3. Daily Logs & Signals

4. Autopilot Shift

The engine evaluates logs at the end of each phase. It computes metrics like Resistance Rating or Completion Consistency to dynamically alter triggers and rules.

Behavioral Safety

Built-in Empathy & Safety

Healthee stands as a safe space in behavioral habit tailorship. No diet culture, BMI indexes, or restriction.

Physical Constraints

Safely scales and adapts if the user reports physical concerns or fatigue history (e.g. joint pain, sleep issues).

Stress-Free Boundaries

We focus on neutral observations and direct consistency guidelines without pushing toxic restriction goals.
