Trust, Safety & Compliance

Compliance is part
of the product.

Claims create obligations. The strategy: wellness-first, clinically conservative, compliance-aware, progressively validated.

Mitigations in place

Wellness-first positioning
FDA enforcement discretion for self-management software without specific treatment suggestions
Bounded skill architecture
3 defined skills prevent diagnostic drift. Consistent with WHO and NIST guidance.
Privacy-by-design
Explicit consent, GDPR-aligned, minimal permissions for HealthKit / Health Connect
Safety layer & escalation
Hard-coded crisis pathways, red-flag escalation, refusal policies
Full auditability
Log prompt version, model, source context, output category per generation

Regulatory landscape

🇺🇸 United States
FDA discretion for lower-risk self-management. FTC Health Breach Notification Rule applies. GoodRx precedent = real risk for ad-based health data sharing.
🇪🇺 European Union
AI Act broadly applicable August 2026. MDR/IVDR classification by intended purpose. EHDS in force March 2025. GDPR: health data is special category.
Medical-data interpretation = educational only
Lab explanation · question prep for clinical visits · care navigation — never diagnosis.
